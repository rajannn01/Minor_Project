About Us

Welcome to Mood-Based Book Recommendation System, an AI-powered platform designed to enhance your reading experience by recommending books that align with your emotions.

In today's fast-paced world, finding the right book that suits your mood can be challenging. Whether you're feeling happy, sad, or in need of motivation, our system leverages emotion detection technology and machine learning to suggest books that resonate with your current emotional state.

Who We Are

We are a team of Computer Engineering Students from Lalitpur Engineering College, Nepal, passionate about Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Human-Computer Interaction. Our project is developed as part of our Bachelor of Engineering curriculum, aiming to make book discovery more personalized and intuitive.

What We Do

  • AI-Powered Emotion Detection: Our system captures and analyzes facial expressions using MediaPipe and a Neural Network Model (MLP) to determine your mood.
  • Smart Book Recommendations: Based on your detected mood, we suggest books from a well-curated database stored in MongoDB.
  • User Engagement: You can rate recommendations, request new suggestions, and track your previous moods for a seamless reading journey.

Our Vision

We believe that books have the power to inspire, heal, and transform lives. Our goal is to use cutting-edge technology to create a platform that understands human emotions and helps people find the perfect book at the right moment.
